Ghana

Ghana, officially known as the Republic of Ghana, is a West African country situated along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. It shares borders with Ivory Coast to the west, Burkina Faso to the north, and Togo to the east. Covering an area of 239,567 square kilometers (92,497 square miles), Ghana features a variety of ecosystems, ranging from coastal savannas to tropical rainforests. With a population of nearly 35 million, it is the second-most populous nation in West Africa. Accra serves as the capital and largest city, while other major urban centers include Kumasi, Tamale, and Sekondi-Takoradi.
